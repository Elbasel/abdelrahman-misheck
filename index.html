John Cale

Some info about John Cale

John Cale (born 1970) is a United States Secret Service special agent and former Capitol Police officer. On October 4, 2014, Cale saved President James Sawyer's life.

Cale was sent to Afghanistan to fight al-Qaeda and the Taliban during the early 2000s, leaving his daughter, Emily behind. During the war he displayed heroism, and even saved Corporal Dawson, nephew of Eli Raphelson, from a burning Humvee as a result of an IED explosion. He received the Silver Star .
